# **App Name**: Medic Teaching Group Landing Page

## Core Features:

- Informational Header: Displays the company logo, navigation links (Inicio, LASEEV 10 EN 1, Tratamientos, Cursos, Contacto), a prominent 'MISTER DOG' button, and the phone number.
- Hero Section: Showcases the company's title and subtitle, a call-to-action button ('CONOCE EL LÁSER 10 EN 1'), and key features such as 'Máquina Alta Gama', 'Doble Longitud de Onda', 'Garantía 2 Años', and 'Formación Profesional'.
- Star Product Showcase: Highlights the 'LASEEV Pro' with a description, prominent pricing ('$7100 USD'), a list of 10 key functions with icons, and a call-to-action button ('SOLICITAR FICHA TÉCNICA').
- Aesthetic Treatments Display: Presents various aesthetic treatments (Endolifting Facial Láser, LipoMela, Armonización Facial, Faloplastia, Rinomodelación) in visually appealing cards, along with a 'AGENDA TU CITA' button.
- High Specialty Courses Section: Lists high-specialty courses (Endoláser, Botox, Armonización Facial, Vascular Facial, Faloplastia, MELA Corporal) with a 'VER FECHAS Y MATRICULARME' call to action.
- Mister Dog Section: Advertises canine services with offers like 'Baño y Corte S/40' and a 'Curso Básico de Peluquería Canina', along with location and phone number information.
- Contact Form: Gathers user information (Name, Phone, Motive) and sends an email.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sophisticated teal (#006263), based on the corporate style, used for main buttons, headings, and details, brings professionalism.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) provides a clean, professional backdrop that doesn't distract from the content.
- Accent color: Soft light green (#90EE90) is used to highlight important information or call-to-action elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Montserrat' (sans-serif) gives the site a modern, clean, and professional aesthetic.
- Use minimalist and professional icons that complement the medical and high-end style.
- Employs a responsive layout using Flexbox/Grid, ensuring adaptability across devices, along with sticky navigation and smooth scrolling.
- Use subtle, elegant animations on transitions to make it feel smooth.
